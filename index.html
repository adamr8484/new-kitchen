The hand painting process

  • First, remove all handles and knobs from doors and drawers.
  • Then remove all doors and drawers.
  • Thoroughly clean panels, doors, drawers and plinths with a specialised degreaser prior to hand painting.
  • All imperfections to be filled and fixed.
  • If you wish to supply new handles or knobs you must indicate that before the sanding to allow for any old holes to be filled where necessary. There is no ‘“extra charge” for this.
  • The kitchen is sanded with a heavy duty sander with dust extraction
  • If it’s a new build kitchen then 2 coats of undercoat is applied
  • 2 coats of paint to be carefully applied
  • If you wish additional layers of varnish are applied for extra protection and gloss/satin finish
  • Paint is left to dry.
  • Reassemble kitchen, fitting new or existing handles and knobs.
