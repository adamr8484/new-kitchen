The hand painting process

  • Remove all handles and knobs from doors and drawers.
  • Then remove all doors and drawers and place them on the racks (If you wish to exchange handles or knobs for new ones, you must indicate that before the sanding process, because it allows me to fill any old holes where necessary. There is no ‘“extra charge” for this).
  • If you wish to supply new handles or knobs you must indicate that before the sanding to allow for any old holes to be filled where necessary. There is no ‘“extra charge” for this.
  • Clean all surfaces with a special degreaser prior to hand painting.
  • Fill and fix all imperfections.
  • The kitchen is sanded with a heavy duty sander with dust extraction
  • Apply 2 coats of undercoat if it is a new build kitchen.
  • Apply 2 coats of paint.
  • Leave the paint to dry.
  • If you wish additional layers of varnish are applied for extra protection and gloss/satin finish
  • Reassemble kitchen, fit the new or existing handles and knobs.
